Background

Hey everyone, I'm Tanner! I've been an entrepreneur since I was 8 years old — my first "business" was selling golf balls on the side of the road. My first real swing came when I joined the founding team at Spiff, which was later acquired by Salesforce.

Since then, I've built and led teams across Operations, Sales, CX, and Partnerships — most recently as VP of Sales and Partnerships at Sendoso, where I helped shape strategy and scale growth across global markets. Along the way, I've also invested in and advised early-stage companies, including a few acquired by firms like HubSpot and HG Insights.

I've learned that success isn't about luck — it's about character, genuine relationships, and hard work. I get energy from connecting great people, learning from them, and pushing together to do meaningful work.
