AI Playlist

By Jeron Paul January 18, 2025

Music has always been a mirror of our times. As we navigate the AI revolution, certain tracks capture the essence of our digital transformationâ€”the excitement, the uncertainty, the raw energy of technological change.

This playlist explores the intersection of artificial intelligence, technology, and human creativity. From the electronic beats of Big Data to the atmospheric soundscapes of deadmau5, each track tells a story about our relationship with technology. Most of them end badly (sorry, haha) but somehow they are still fun. And I feel like the power of the creativity in the music is it's own powerful assertion of human force against the machine.

Fun fact: The Veldt by Deadmau5 is based on the a short story by Ray Bradbury called "The Veldt" about the risks of technology and the dangers of automation.

Whether you're coding, building, or just contemplating the future, these songs provide the perfect soundtrack for the AI era.
